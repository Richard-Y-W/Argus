# HYP-014 — Truncated historical coverage attenuates measured US publication decay

*Registered 2026-07-17 before the restricted-window outcomes were computed. AI-led.*

## Motivation and prior

EXP-013 could only observe JKP world-ex-US returns mainly from 1986, while the C&Z US panel begins in 1926. If missing early original-sample returns mechanically compress the in-sample benchmark, imposing the same 1986 floor on C&Z should materially attenuate EXP-001's post-publication coefficient.

## Registered predictions

- **P1 primary:** the 1986-floor post-publication coefficient remains negative with |t| at least 2.0, but its absolute magnitude is at least 25% smaller than EXP-001's full-history coefficient.
- **P2:** the restricted post-publication coefficient is no more negative than -0.10 percentage points per month.
- **P3:** the restricted post-publication-minus-post-sample contrast remains negative.
- **P4 robustness:** P1's attenuation direction holds with a 1990 floor and among clearly replicated predictors.
- **Falsifier:** the 1986-floor coefficient is equally or more negative than the contemporaneously recomputed full-history coefficient, or reverses sign.

## Data and internal debate

Use the pinned C&Z October 2025 predictor panel and EXP-001 definitions. Recompute the full panel, then drop observations before 1986-01 for the primary restriction, reapply the 12-month in-sample/post-publication eligibility rule, and estimate predictor fixed effects with month-clustered errors. This changes coverage, not portfolio construction or factor identity.

- **Optimist:** a clean attenuation would explain part of the international gap without inventing a geographic mechanism.
- **Skeptic:** later-starting signals remain eligible, so this is not a balanced matched sample.
- **Statistician:** compare coefficients generated by one script and expose sample flow.
- **Economist:** attenuation is a measurement result, not evidence against learning.
- **Portfolio manager:** gross characteristic returns remain non-executable evidence.
- **ML researcher:** fix both date floors and thresholds before execution.

Expected failure probability is 45%. The test has moderate learning value and low direct alpha value.
