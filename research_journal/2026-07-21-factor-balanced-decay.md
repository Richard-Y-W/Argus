# 2026-07-21 — Equal factor weights preserve US publication decay

An AI-led cycle replaced pooled factor-month weighting with one contrast per eligible factor. The equal-factor estimate remained -0.154 pp/month, and 70.9% of factors were negative. Unequal histories therefore do not carry the standalone US result, although dependence among related factors remains a limitation.
