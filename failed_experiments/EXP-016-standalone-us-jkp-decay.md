# EXP-016 — Standalone US JKP publication decay

**Not supported.** Standalone US post-publication decay is -0.164 percentage points per month (t = -2.97), but the preregistered direct US-minus-world-ex-US gap is -0.131 pp/month with t = -1.45, below the required 1.65. Strong US decay is established within this design; a statistically distinguishable geography gap is not.
