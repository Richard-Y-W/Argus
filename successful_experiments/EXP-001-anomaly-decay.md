# EXP-001 — McLean & Pontiff three-window replication ✓

*Completed 2026-07-10. Full materials: `experiments/EXP-001-anomaly-decay/` (design, code, results, figures).*

**One line:** On Chen–Zimmermann open data (212 predictors, 1926–2024), published US predictor returns fall 36.8% post-sample and 55.1% post-publication but remain at 0.333%/mo gross (t = 7.6) — the M&P partial-decay pattern replicates, with all three registered predictions inside their pre-committed ranges and six specs in agreement.

**Also filed:** first entry in `replications/` territory — logged here because the registered predictions passed; the replication write-up lives with the experiment.

**Caveats that travel with the result:** gross of costs; annual publication dating attenuates the publication effect; unplanned observation of a 2020–2024 partial revival flagged, not interpreted.
