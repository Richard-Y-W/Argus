# EXP-010 — Within-signal weighting and publication decay

**Rejected 2026-07-15.** The paired VW-minus-EW proportional-decay estimate was +0.141 but imprecise (t = 1.76), the levels discriminator had the wrong sign, and only 50% of signals favored greater VW decay. Pairing removed EXP-009's between-signal composition confound without establishing weighting as a mechanism proxy.
